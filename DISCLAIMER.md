# Disclaimer

**RightCall is a personal demonstration project.** It is not a product, not a
compliance tool, and not affiliated with, endorsed by, or based on any
employer's data, processes, criteria, or systems.

- **All data is synthetic.** Every member, provider, plan, clinical detail,
  internal criterion, and coverage request in this repository was invented for
  this project. Any resemblance to real people or real plan documents is
  coincidental.
- **Regulatory content is simplified.** The rule summaries in `data/` are
  plain-language paraphrases written for this project in the spirit of public
  CMS rules (decision timeframes, coverage-criteria precedence, QMB
  protections, skilled-maintenance coverage). They are illustrative, not
  authoritative, and must not be used for actual coverage decisions,
  compliance, legal, or clinical purposes.
- **RightCall does not make coverage decisions.** It demonstrates an
  architecture in which software supports a human reviewer with deterministic
  checks and sourced context. Its policy layer can only move a case toward
  human review, never toward denial or approval.
- **No PHI.** No protected health information was used in building or testing
  this project.

If you are a Medicare member with a coverage question, contact your plan, 1-800-MEDICARE,
or your State Health Insurance Assistance Program (SHIP).
